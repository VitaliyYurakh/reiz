ELIGIBILITY FOR EXTENDED COVERAGE

  • Drivers aged 25+ with at least 4 years of experience.

  • Rental period must be from 3 days and longer.

  • The person on the rental agreement must be the one driving.

  • In case of an accident, notify the company within one hour and call the police.

NOT COVERED & PROHIBITED

  • Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs.

  • Allowing an unauthorized driver. Third parties at the wheel.

  • Theft of external parts such as mirrors or wipers.

  • Leaving the scene of an accident.

  • Using the car for commercial purposes (taxi, sub-rental). Strictly prohibited.

  • Damage to the vehicle’s interior.
